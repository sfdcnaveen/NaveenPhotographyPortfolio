I never planned to capture the world.
I just didn’t want to miss it.

So whenever something feels a little more alive—
a color, a shadow, a passing face—
I pause,
and I keep it.

Not for anyone else,
not for perfection,
just for the simple joy of holding onto a moment
that would have otherwise disappeared.

This is my kind of art—
unplanned, imperfect, and quietly mine.
